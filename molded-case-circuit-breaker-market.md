Electrical circuits are shielded from various forms of harm by molded case circuit breakers, which are designed to prevent short circuits and overloads. MCCBs are typically found in panelboards, switchboards, and other such equipment. They are essential because they prevent harm to both humans and the environment by instantly cutting off power in the event of an issue. These devices can be reset after the issue has been fixed. The need for more dependable power protection equipment is growing, which will increase demand for MCCBs everywhere.
