The Air Start Unit (ASU) market is an integral part of the aviation industry, supplying the necessary compressed air to start aircraft engines on the ground. These units are crucial for the efficient operation of aircraft, particularly in environments where on-board starting systems may be inadequate or unavailable. The market for Air Start Units is expanding as air travel and cargo operations increase globally, driven by the need for efficient ground handling solutions in both civil and military aviation sectors.
