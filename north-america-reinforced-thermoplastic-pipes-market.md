The North America reinforced thermoplastic pipes (RTP) market has gained significant traction in recent years, driven by the increasing demand for advanced piping solutions in various industries. Reinforced thermoplastic pipes are composite pipes made from layers of polymer reinforced with synthetic fibers or metal. These pipes offer several advantages over traditional metal pipes, including corrosion resistance, flexibility, and ease of installation. RTPs are widely used in the oil and gas industry, water distribution, and chemical processing, among others, where durability and performance are critical.
